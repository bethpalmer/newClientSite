Mondays Child UK

Website design and build project for a child focused drama based intervention charity

The Brief

Local charity Monday's Child UK needed a new site to replace their outdated site. They had a strong identity and wanted to retain the edgy urban feel of the site reusing a lot of the same visual elements and content.

The Process

Through client discovery sessions I came to understand the needs of the client, and together we worked out a brief which suited their requirements. We agreed layout, style and implementation before the build commenced.

The Product

This scrolling website is a comtemporary swing on their original site, retaining the urban edgy feel, and using techniques such as collapsible panels to make content more manageable for the user to digest.
