Door 2 Door Coaches

Company logo design and production for a Brighton based private hire coach company

The Brief

This Brighton coach hire company needed a new logo: this was predominantly to display on their new website, but also with a view to printed business stationary, and something that could be developed into a coach wrap in a future round of development. The client liked the idea of 3D text and the colour scheme red, black and white.

The Process

In the designs I trialled different types of 3D text as well as some 2D comparisons and I included plays on doors within a number of designs. The development process was undertaken in 2 distinct phases, both followed by a round of feedback culminating in the final design. Below are a selection of designs from the 2 phases.

The Product

The finished logo product was made available in 3 colour schemes, on each a white, black and red background, fit for a multitude of uses.
