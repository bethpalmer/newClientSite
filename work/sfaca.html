Shehzad Farrukh - SF ACA

Website design and build project for a London based accountancy firm.

The Brief

Shehzad Farrukh is a London based accountant. He was in need of an effective marketing website and someone to manage his hosting and email continuity. He provided a business card with simple branding and colour scheme requesting design continuity from the website.

The Process

We agreed upon a scrolling website design. Through existing site analysis and discovery sessions, I understood the image the business was portraying, and extrapolated the themes to work with as: city, approachable, efficient, clean and warm. Visuals were sourced and approved. The site was built and accepted as is by a very happy client.

The Product

I provide a hosting agreement with this client including continuity of their domain email through gmail and have advised on their marketing strategy moving forwards.
