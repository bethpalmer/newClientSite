Shehzad Farrukh - SF ACA

Website design and build project for a London based accountancy firm.

The Brief

Shehzad Farrukh is a London based accountant, who needed a website he could use to market his services effectively. He provided a business card with logo requesting design continuity. He wanted someone who could manage his hosting and email continuity moving forwards.

The Process

We agreed upon a scrolling website design. Through exisiting site analysis and understanding the business image, we extrapolated the themes of city, approachable, efficient, clean and warm. Visuals were sourced and approved. The site was built and accepted as is by a very happy client.

The Product

I have a hosting agreement with this client including continuity of their domain email through gmail. I have advised on their marketing strategy moving forwards.
