Essential Blends

Website design and build project for a Brighton based holistic therapist with a focus on her natural skincare range.

The Brief

Sophie Joseph, a local holistic therapist required a simple landing page focused on promoting her 100% natural luxury skincare products. The goals were to direct customers to her etsy shop, act as a hub connecting her various social media presences as well as legitimise the brand. The page needed to be easily extendable over the coming months to potentially include things like an etsy plugin. The client requested use of the wordpess platform.

The Process

Through discovery sessions I came to understand Sophie's business needs and requirements. We agreed a theme, and broad spectrum layout and content, the copy of which I edited to suit the layout.

The Product

The final product meets the brief and is able to be fully extended in the next round of development.
