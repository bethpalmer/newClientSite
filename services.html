Services

Website Design

Your website is the corner stone of your online presence. It needs to legitimise and effectively market your business to new customers as well as providing all of the useful information your customers expect in a user friendly way.

I work on a case by case basis when it comes to web design, after all, the most important thing about your site is that it tailored to suit you and your specific needs and business targets. However, the workflow that underpin all web development projects is the same.

  1. Getting to know you and your business, your goals, your target market and USPs
  2. Getting to know your market, any design trends, identifying what works, and where you can deviate from the norm to create a product which really stand out.
  3. Agree the broad spectrum content, layout, visuals and theme and then get cracking with the development work.

Communication is is a really crucial element to good design which is why the planning process is so important. It’s important to me that we are both comfortable to challenge each other on content and design decisions to ensure that together we create the best possible product for your customers.

As a keen copywriter I am likely to recommend content edits and formatting in line with proven marketing principles and effective layout as it has proven to be very helpful on many projects for clients to receive subjective feedback on website content.

I normally recommend a bespoke system, including a Perch CMS to allow you to make edits independently, or alternatively I can work on a platform like wordpress where needed.

Once the product has been developed, a single round of feedback should enable us to finalise a product which is beautifully effective in achieving its goals.

Projects start from as little as £100/page depending on the requirements. I ask for 25% of the project payment upfront, and the rest either in instalments (depending on the length of the project) or on completion, prior to facilitating hosting for the site.

My hourly rate is reduced from £40/hr to £25/hr for those who sign up for a subsequent hosting and support contract.

Graphic Design

Your company branding and logo design is key in communicating your companies core values to your customers.

Communication is also key in designing a great logo and brand which is why it’s important for me to really know your business.

My logo design process is:

  1. Discovery - learning about you as a company as well as the market as a whole.
  2. Initial development and feedback.
  3. Second stage development and feedback.
  4. Production of the final production with variations in a range of file formats

Additional branding services can then be tagged onto this package to suit your exact requirements.

My logo design package costs £280 or £180 for customers who are signed up with a hosting and support contract, with further services available for my hourly rate which is reduced from £40/hr to £25/hr for customers on a contract.
