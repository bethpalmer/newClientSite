About Me

Hi, I'm Beth

I am a Brighton based website designer and web support provider. I'm passionate about great web design and love helping individuals and SMEs by developing beautiful effective websites tailored to meet their specific business needs.

My talent lies in understanding what you want to get across and translating that into powerful content. I employ layout and presentation which is designed to guide your users into undertaking predetermined actions, increasing user engagement and customer conversion rates for your business.

I provide a comprehensive design service so that the development process is minimal hassle for you. I also offer a full package of hosting, email and support with a personal and strategic service to keep your site up and running and at peak efficiency.
